Dr. Evelyn Tai

Dr Evelyn Tai is a Fellow of the Royal College of Ophthalmologists and the International Council of Ophthalmology. As the postgraduate coordinator of her university’s Masters of Medicine in Ophthalmology program and an examiner the Malaysian Universities Conjoint Committee of Ophthalmologists Examinations, she is active in teaching, training, supervision and assessment.She is also a keen researcher, holding several grants and publishing more than 30 articles to date. Besides that, she is a volunteer with the Malaysian Medical Relief Society and hopes to increase her involvement in international humanitarian work, especially in the field of preventable blindness. She is currently exploring opportunities to further her fellowship training.
